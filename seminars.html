    • arXiv I interested everyday

    [ArXiv:2210.12164]Studying Cosmic Dawn using redshifted HI 21-cm signal: A brief review

    by Ankita Bera et al.

  • They discuss different phases of cosmic dawn as the ambient gas and the background radiations evolve with redshift.
  • They address the consequences of several possible heating sources and radiation background on the global 21-cm signal.

    • [ArXiv:2210.13335]Holographic energy density, dark energy sound speed, and tensions in cosmological parameters: H0 and S8

    by Wilmar Cardona and M. A. Sabogal

  • DE sound speed plays a part in the matter clustering behaviour through its effect on the evolution of the potential potential.
  • CMB + CMB lensing + redshift-space-distortions + local distance-ladder + SNe + BAO
  • \(\hat{c}_s^2 = 1\) is excluded at \( \gtrsim 3 \sigma \).
  • They found \(H_0, S_8 \) in good agreement (within \(\approx 2 \sigma \)) with low redshift probes.
  • But the baryon energy density tension with BBN constraints.

    • [ArXiv:2210.13436]Microlensing of gravitational waves by dark matter structures

    by Malcolm Fairbairn et al.

  • The mircrolensing effect on gravitational waves singals from black hole binaries induced by low-mass dark matter halos that do not retain enough baryonic matter to hold stars.
  • Detectability.

    • [ArXiv:2210.13006]Testing general relativity with TianQin: the prospect of using the inspiral signals of black hole binaries

    by Changfu Shi et al.

  • The parameterized post-Einsteinian (ppE) waveform.
  • Black hole binaries with total masses ranging from \(10 M_\odot \sim 10^7 M_\odot\).
  • The improvement at the -4PN and 2PN orders can be about 13 and 3 orders of magnitude (compare to the results from GW150914).
