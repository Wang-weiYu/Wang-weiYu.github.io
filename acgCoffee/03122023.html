arXiv I interested everyday

[ArXiv:2311.17988]A simultaneous solution to the Hubble tension and observed bulk flow within 250 h−1 Mpc

by Sergij Mazurenko et al.

Their results suggest that several cosmological tensions can be simultaneously resolved if structure grows more efficiently than in the ΛCDM paradigm on scales of tens to hundreds of Mpc.

[ArXiv:2311.18443]The Hubble Tension Survey: A Statistical Analysis of the 2012-2022 Measurements

by Bao Wang et al.

These statistical results indicate that the underestimation of error bars for H0 remains prevalent over the past decade, dominated by systematic errors in the methodologies of CMB and local distance ladder analyses.

[ArXiv:2311.18546]Parametric resonance of gravitational waves in general scalar-tensor theories

by Yi-Fu Cai et al.

the resonant peaks in the spectral density may be detectable by forthcoming detectors such as LISA, Taiji, Einstein Telescope and Cosmic Explorer.

[ArXiv:2311.18032]Gravitational wave signals from long lasting binary-single black hole encounters

by Elena Codazzo et al.

Using the ARWV N-body code, they performed 42 numerical simulations, to explore various outcomes of binary-single interaction, and they characterize the diverse GW signatures produced during these encounters.
