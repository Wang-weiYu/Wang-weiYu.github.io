arXiv I interested everyday

[ArXiv:2312.09550]Electromagnetic field in a cavity induced by gravitational waves

by Danho Ahn et al.

  • the rigid condition imposed on the material, as provided in the literature, is inappropriate due to its reliance on a gauge-dependent quantity that cannot be controlled experimentally.
  • incorporate elasticity into the material and revise the governing equations for the electric field induced by GWs, expressing them solely in terms of gauge-invariant quantities.

[ArXiv:2312.09712]Geometric conservation in curved spacetime and entropy

by Sinya Aoki et al.

  • an improved definition of new conserved quantities derived from the energy-momentum tensor in curved spacetime by introducing an additional scalar function.
  • the conserved current and the associated conserved charge become geometric under a certain initial condition of the scalar function, and show that such a conserved geometric current generally exists in curved spacetime.
  • the geometric conserved current agrees with the entropy current for the perfect fluid, thus the conserved charge is the total entropy of the system.

[ArXiv:2312.09814]Review of Hubble tension solutions with new SH0ES and SPT-3G data

by Ali Rida Khalife et al.

  • improve over previous works in several ways.
  • AxiCLASSA new version of CLASS that can deal with axion-like particles. Co-developped with Tristan L. Smith, Tanvi Karwal + some initial commits by Charlotte Owen.

[ArXiv:2312.09853]Extra Dimensions Beyond the Horizon

by Jeffrey Kuntz and Andreas Trautner

4D brane tensions that that must be raising towards the fundamental scale of the model, while the observable 4D expansion rate is decreasing.

[ArXiv:2312.09875]The observer-dependence of the Hubble parameter: a covariant perspective

by Roy Maartens et al.

  • a model-independent view of the Hubble parameter in a general spacetime.

[ArXiv:2312.09458]Model-independent confirmation of a constant speed of light over cosmological distances

by Fulvio Melia
